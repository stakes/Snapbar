Snapbar

Your last 5 screenshots
in your macOS menu bar.

Snapbar hangs around and waits for you to take screenshots. It helps keep your desktop nice and tidy by squirreling screenshots away for you. It also makes them easy to find later on from your menu bar.

It has a quiet but firm opinion that most of us really only need the last few screenshots we took. So it keeps a clean machine by holding onto your last five screenshots and automatically deleting older ones.
